AI Transformation Roadmap

Moving from "Process Mapping" to "Process Automation." Identifying high-value opportunities to deploy AI Agents against shadow work.

1. Procurement & Sourcing Predictive Supply

Current Shadow Work

  • Manually reviewing Excel history to guess forecast.
  • Typing POs into ERP.
  • Chasing vendors for tracking numbers via email.

The AI Agent

Predictive Buying Agent

Analyzes seasonality, sales velocity, and lead times to draft POs automatically. Scrapes vendor portals to update ETAs in real-time.

The Human Role

Strategic Sourcing

Negotiating better terms, finding new suppliers, and handling "Stock Out" emergencies.

2. Inventory Management Visual Intelligence

Current Shadow Work

  • Walking aisles to count boxes (Cycle Counting).
  • Investigating "Why is this missing?" (Data digging).
  • Keying in location updates.

The AI Agent

Visual Audit Drone / Cam

Fixed cameras or drones scan barcodes overnight. AI compares visual stock vs. ERP data and flags variances automatically.

The Human Role

Exception Handler

Only investigating the variances the AI flags. Optimizing warehouse layout.

3. Sales & Order Entry Zero-Touch Entry

Current Shadow Work

  • Opening PDF Purchase Orders from email.
  • Manually typing SKU codes into Sales Orders.
  • Checking credit limits manually.

The AI Agent

Inbox-to-ERP Agent

LLM reads the PDF/Email body, extracts SKUs/Qty, checks stock, and creates the Sales Order draft instantly.

The Human Role

Relationship Manager

Upselling, consulting on technical specs, and managing client relationships.

4. Finance (AP & AR) Autonomous Audit

Current Shadow Work

  • Staring at screens to match PO vs Invoice.
  • Sending "Please pay me" emails.
  • Reconciling bank statements row-by-row.

The AI Agent

3-Way Match & Collections Bot

Auto-approves matches within tolerance (e.g., <$0.05 diff). Writes personalized, polite-but-firm collections emails based on payment history.

The Human Role

CFO / Controller

Managing cash flow strategy (Treasury), handling disputes, and securing credit lines.
