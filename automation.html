AI Transformation Roadmap

A consolidated view of all high-value automation opportunities across the 5 core pillars.

1. Procurement Strategic

Predictive Buying Agent

Shadow Work: Reviewing Excel history, guessing forecasts.

AI Solution: Analyzes seasonality & velocity to draft POs automatically.

Vendor Scorecard Bot

Shadow Work: Manually tracking "Who was late?" via email.

AI Solution: Grades vendors on OTIF and auto-negotiates penalties.

Global Price Arbitrage

Shadow Work: Googling parts to see if you're overpaying.

AI Solution: Scrapes global spot markets to flag contract overpricing.

2. Inventory Operational

Visual Audit Drone

Shadow Work: Walking aisles to count boxes (Cycle Counting).

AI Solution: Drones/Cameras scan barcodes overnight and flag variances.

Smart Slotting Engine

Shadow Work: Walking miles to pick popular items stored far away.

AI Solution: "Move iPhone 15s to the front" based on pick velocity.

Obsolescence Predictor

Shadow Work: Writing off dead stock that sat for 12 months.

AI Solution: Predicts "Dead in 60 days" for early discounting.

3. Sales Revenue

Inbox-to-ERP Agent

Shadow Work: Typing PDF orders into the system manually.

AI Solution: Reads emails/PDFs and drafts Sales Order instantly.

4. Fulfillment Logistics

Pick Path Optimization

Shadow Work: Walking inefficient zig-zag routes.

AI Solution: Calculates mathematical shortest path for picking.

3D Box Calculator

Shadow Work: Guessing box size and shipping "air".

Predictive Carrier Select

Shadow Work: Defaulting to one carrier out of habit.

5. Finance Control

Autonomous 3-Way Match

Shadow Work: Staring at screens to match PO vs Invoice.

AI Solution: Auto-approves matches within tolerance (<$0.05).

Cash Flow Oracle

Shadow Work: Reacting to cash surprises.

AI Solution: Predicts cash crunch 2 weeks out based on real AP/AR dates.

Fraud Sentinel

Shadow Work: Manual spot checks for fake invoices.
